# Din Kardeşim — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** November 09, 2025

This document explains how the Din Kardeşim mobile application processes user data. By using the application, you are deemed to have accepted this privacy policy.

## Data Collected

- No personal identity information is collected.
- The application does not request email, phone number, address, or any personally identifiable information.
- The app does not request access to location, camera, microphone, or other device permissions.
- No cookies, advertising identifiers, or user-tracking mechanisms are used.

## Usage Data

- In-app interactions may be collected anonymously for statistical or technical analysis.
- Collected data cannot be used to identify a user and is not combined with any other data sources.

## Third-Party Sharing

- No personal data is shared with third parties.
- The application does not use third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking software.
- Hosting providers may store anonymized technical logs such as IP and device information for security and debugging purposes.

## Data Security

- Any stored data is used only for system performance and error logs.
- Logs are protected against unauthorized access.
- Logs and temporary data are periodically deleted.

## Children’s Privacy

- The app is suitable for all ages.
- No personal information is collected from children.

## Changes to This Policy

- The policy may be updated when necessary.
- The latest version will be accessible from within the application.

## Contact

- Developer contact email can be provided upon request.
